Roger Federer

An ode to, in my opinion, the greatest athlete there has ever been.

You know the guy was pretty special when John McEnroe has this to say about him:

“[Federer] ...is the most beautiful man to watch play tennis. The most beautiful I've ever seen play. His movement, combined with the artistry, his racket, the look, he's got everything going.”

A career spanning over 24 years, over a 100 ATP titles, a whopping 20 grand slams and best of all, an unerring grace and artistry that endeared him to sports fans of all ages. An introverted and shy genius from Basel, very few, if any have been or would be able to capture the hearts of tennis fans as Federer did. A brutal attacking style of play, combined with flawless elegance and strokes of unmatchable genius, Federer was, simply invincible at his best.
